Rules of Global Trader Competition


On weekly basis all participants will receive funds for trading. This money will be given by Membrana platform with the use of its unique algorithm of trust management.

Details of the Competition:


Global Traders Competition will start 15 June 2018.
All traders will receive 0.012 btc each ($100). Deposit are available for trading only on Membrana platform and work through our smart-contract. Trading will be provided on Binance exchange...
After each week - we will conclude results and give rewards for traders.

All traders will receive their earnings on Ethereum wallet and official Membrana hoodie.
Top5 participants will receive Tournament point depending on their result position.
Those Tournament point can be achieved every week and seen in the result list on Membrana official website.

After 12 rounds of weekly competition we will conclude the results of 1st Global Trading Competition and reward each participant.
All Tournament points will be converted into Membrana Tоkеns.
Best 20 traders in the global ranking will receive a part of $100,000 prize pool!

How to participate?


To participate, you need to pass the KYC procedure, available on our official website - Membrana.io/competition

Then you need to login on our platform using your Ethereum wallet. You don’t need an account on exchange or anything else!

After verifying your account we will place you in the tournament schedule. For first weekly rounds we limit the amount of participants to 100, but in case there will be no space, you can be sure, that you will have a chance to compete in future rounds. Also, need to be mentioned, that you can participate in multiple rounds of Global Trader Competition to earn as many Tournament Points as you can!

Good luck to everyone, and let the competition begin!
